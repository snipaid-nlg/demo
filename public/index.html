How it works

Text is generated with a language model from the family of Generative Pretrained Transformers (GPT). GPT models take a text string and predict the tokens that are most likely to follow.

The Model: gptj-title-teaser-10k

Our model is built on GPT-J, an Open Source Model from the GPT family. We trained GPT-J-6B-8bit on 10.000 German News Articles, balanced by 10 news categories and randomized over different news publishers and multiple years. You can download the PyTorch Model Checkpoints from here. To run the model you need ~11 GB GPU memory.

Limitations

When generating text with the model please keep in mind, that the statistically most likely next token is often not the token that produces the most "accurate" text. Never depend upon those models to produce factually accurate output! We recommend having a human curate or filter the outputs before releasing them, both to censor undesirable content and to improve the quality of the results. See also limitations and biases of GPT-J.
